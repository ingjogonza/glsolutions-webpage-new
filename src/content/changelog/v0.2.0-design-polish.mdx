## Improvements

- **Dark mode contrast pass** - lifted `--color-text-secondary-dark` from `#a8b5c8` to `#c5cedb` for better body-copy readability at 14-16px sizes
- **Primary in dark mode** - brightened `--color-primary-dark` from `#3478d4` to `#3884eb` so links stay readable against the deep navy canvas
- **Softer card edges** - dark-mode card borders moved from `neutral-800` to `neutral-700`
- **AnimatedText tuning** - tightened the hero text entrance timing and reduced first-paint overhead
- **AOS staggering** - standardized reveal sequences around predictable delay steps

## New component

- **`BrowserFrame.astro`** - the Mac-style window chrome around product previews, extracted into a reusable component with `url`, `variant`, and `className` props

## Fixes

- Header z-index conflict with FAQ accordion on mobile Safari
- `prefers-reduced-motion` handling for text entrances
- TOC sticky offset on wide article layouts

## Release status

`v0.2` made the template feel more coherent, but the content architecture and final SaaS story were still being shaped for `v1.0`.
